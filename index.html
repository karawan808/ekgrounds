The Introduction

So in case you were wondering what this is all about ...

EKG Rounds is an active learning tool allowing you to publish, comment, interact, and share ideas within an active community of licensed physicians, fellows, residents, medical students and other healthcare professionals.

It's intuitive, based on grassroots analysis, and built solely on non-profit interactions of talented people who are helping each other prosper.

Features

Interactive ECG readings at your fingertips

Comment and add important details, be active!

Together, all these interactions make up the precious nuggets of knowledge that can be used by various people all over the world.

Rewards and Reputation

Making correct diagnoses and sharing accurate analyses will raise your reputation score, allow you to earn titles, eventually granting you higher precedence in rounds.

Diagnose

Easily publish your ideas within the community and receive feedback.

Collaborate

Freely discuss, share, and learn with thousands of participants using EKGrounds.

Learn

Tag relevant EKGs and place them in your library. For example, an EKG tagged under AV block can be placed within its own subcategory for your future reference,

Tutor mode

Test mode will hide the diagnosis and allow you to test yourself or teach others.
